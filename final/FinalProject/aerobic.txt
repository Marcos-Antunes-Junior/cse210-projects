NAME: Jumping Jack -- DESCRIPTION: Stand upright with your legs together, arms at your sides. Bend your knees slightly, and jump into the air. As you jump, spread your legs to be about shoulder-width apart. Stretch your arms out and over your head. Jump back to starting position.|
NAME: Jogging in Place -- DESCRIPTION: Lift your right arm and left foot at the same time. Raise your knee as high as your hips. Then switch to the opposite foot, quickly lifting your right foot to hip height. At the same time, move your right arm back and your left arm forward and up.|
